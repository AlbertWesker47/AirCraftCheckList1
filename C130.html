Lockheed Martin C-130 Check List

HYDRAULIC PANEL ................ SET (EMERGENCY BRAKES AND ALL PUMPS ON)
PARKING BRAKE ................ SET, PRESSURE CHECKED, REMOVE CHOCKS
THROTTLES ................ GROUND IDLE
CHOCKS ................ REMOVED
BEFORE STARTING ENGINES CHECK ................ COMPLETE
STARTING ENGINES
CLEAR/START #3 ENGINE ................ OK
REMOVE EXTERNAL EQUIPEMENT (GRD PWR CART) ................ OK
CLEAR/START #4 ENGINE ................ OK
EXTERNAL EQUIPMENT................REMOVED
CLEAR/START #2 ENGINE ................ OK
CLEAR/START #1 ENGINE ................ OK
SECURE CREW ENTRANCE DOOR ... (LOADMASTER)
BEFORE TAXI
COMPASS SYSTEM AND HEADING INDICATORS ................ CHECKED AND VERIFIED
RADIOS AND NAVIGATION ................. CHECKED(ALL NECESSARY EQUIPMENT ON)
IFF/TRANSPONDER ........................................... CHECKED/STANDBY
FLAPS ............................................... SET 50% (FOR TAKEOFF)
HYDRAULIC PRESSURES ............................................... CHECKED
AUTOPILOT ...................................................... VERIFY OFF
INSTRUMENTS ....................................................... CHECKED
SCNS/GPS .......................................................... CHECKED
ALTIMETERS ............................................. SET(LOCAL SETTING)
TAXI LIGHT ............................................................. ON
TAXI
BRAKES................ (emergency then switch/check normal)
INSTRUMENTS ................ (PROPER MOVEMENT AND DISPLACEMENT DURING LEFT AND RIGHT TURN)
BEFORE TAKEOFF
SAFETY BELT/SHOULDER HARNESS................ FASTENED/UNLOCKED
TRIM TABS ................ SET(FOR TAKEOFF)
FLAPS ................ SET(CONFIRM 50% FOR TAKEOFF)
FLIGHT CONTROLS ................ CHECKED (FREEDOM OF MOVEMENT)
CREW BRIEFING ................ COMPLETED(INCLUDED TOLD,DEPARTURE INSTRUCTIONS, AND EMERGENCIES)
INSTRUMENTS ................ SET(FOR DEPARTURE)
SCNS/GPS ................ ENHANCED INTERRUPTED ALIGNMENT COMPLETE(IF NEEDED)
LINEUP

IFF TRANSPONDER ................ ON/SET
TAKEOFF
HOLD BRAKES AND SET TAKEOFF POWER ................ (100% RPM DO NOT EXCEED MAX TORQUE)
RELEASE BRAKES ................ CHECKED
TAKEOFF SPEED ................ (100-110 KIAS DEPENDING ON WEIGHT)
ROTATE/ESTABLISH 10 DEGREES NOSE UP ................ CHECKED
AFTER TAKEOFF
LANDING GEAR ................ UP(UPON POSITIVE INDICATION OF CLIMB)
FLAPS ................ UP(AFTER GEAR RETRACTS)
TAXI/LANDING LIGHTS ................ OFF AND RETRACT
AUX HYDRAULIC PUMP ................ OFF
LEADING EDGE ANTI-ICE ................ CHECKED
ACCELERATE TO 180 KIAS FOR CLIMB ................ CHECKED
CRUISE

IFF TRANSPONDER ................ ON/SET
